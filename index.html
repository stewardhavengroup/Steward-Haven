Steward Haven

Private Capital & Strategic Growth

We are a consortium of private sponsors dedicated to the strategic advancement and continuity of established service enterprises and outdoor hospitality properties through capital partnership, succession planning, and operational enhancement.

Acquisitions

Selective acquisition of service-based businesses with demonstrated market position and operational excellence. We provide capital and strategic guidance to exceptional management teams.

Equity Partnerships

Minority equity positions in high-potential service enterprises. We align interests through shared ownership while preserving operational autonomy and founder vision.

Strategic Advisory

Ongoing advisory relationships structured through quarterly retainers with nominal equity participation. Access to our network and expertise without dilutive capital requirements.

Our Philosophy

Steward Haven operates with discretion and selectivity. We partner exclusively with service businesses and outdoor hospitality properties—including campgrounds and RV parks—that have proven their concept, established their market, and demonstrate the potential for significant scale. Our sponsors remain anonymous, our involvement strategic, and our commitment long-term.
